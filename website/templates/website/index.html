WHAT IS CCNA EXAM?

-CCNA (Cisco Certified Network Associate) is an IT certification from Cisco.
-The CCNA is designed to validate your knowledge on fundamental networking concepts.
-You can become CCNA certified by taking and passing a single exam – the CCNA 200-301

WHY LEARN CCNA?

The basic concepts of networking such as:
LAN/WAN, TCP/IP model, Switches and routers, Network utilities (ping, tracert, arp), IP addressing and subnetting, VLANs and trunking, Routing protocols such as OSPF, WLAN, NAT and ACLs and Automation and programmability

WHAT YOU WILL LEARN?

-The CCNA certification is accepted all around the world.
-Professionals armed with the coveted CCNA certificate are entitled to get higher paying jobs when compared to their non-certified counterparts.
-CCNA certification processes are enhancing the knowledge base of networking experts – and in many more ways than one!
-The knowledge acquired through the CCNA certificate will help you solve more problems in networking.

FUTURE SCOPE

-CCNA's scope lies in the wide range of software developers and network administrators.CCNA jobs for freshers in recent days have increased with the development in technologies.
-CCNA in India will grow huge because Network automation and programming will take around 70% of focus in any IT organization and anyone with the CCNA, CCNP, CCIE Certifications training will be the ones getting hired.
